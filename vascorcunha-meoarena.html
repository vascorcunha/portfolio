MEO ARENA

MEO 2022

As part of a rebranding, a new identity was created for the arena, accompanied by a series of posters at the entrance. These posters encouraged visitors to truly experience the event and not get distracted by their phones, using playful and pun-filled messages.

Art Direction - Vasco R. Cunha

Identity Design - Vasco R. Cunha

Copywriter - Manuel Stock
